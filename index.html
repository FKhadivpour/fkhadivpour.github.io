About Me

Results-driven Machine Learning Engineer and Data Scientist with 5+ years of experience in deep learning, explainable AI, and generative AI. Proven ability to design, deploy, and optimize AI solutions for real-world applications. Skilled in AI pipeline development, prompt engineering, and RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) using LLMs. Strong background in compute vision and time-series data analysis. Experienced in working in a fast-paced startup environment and agile teams, collaborating with cross-functional groups to develop scalable AI-driven solutions.


• Experienced in developing advanced LLM pipelines using LangChain, LangSmith, and vector databases (e.g., FAISS, Chroma) for retrieval-augmented generation (RAG).
• Familiar with Model Context Protocol (MCP) for enabling agent communication and enhancing interoperability in LLM-driven applications.
• Skilled in deploying deep learning architectures such as Transformers, Diffusion Models, CNNs, and RNNs.
• Extensive hands-on experience with image and sequential data, including ResNet, AlexNet, LSTM, and attention mechanisms.
• Strong foundation in machine learning algorithms including logistic/linear regression, decision trees, KNN, and dimensionality reduction techniques.
• Experienced in designing production-grade ML pipelines, performing end-to-end deployment via Flask APIs, and integrating unstructured data sources (images, PDFs, CSVs).
