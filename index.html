About Me

I am a Machine Learning Researcher and Data Scientist with over 3 years of hands-on experience in developing and deploying advanced ML models. My expertise spans working with image and sequential data, leveraging cutting-edge explainable AI techniques to interpret convolutional neural networks (CNNs) and large language models (LLMs).



• Expertise in different ML algorithms: Linear and logistic regression, decision tree, KNN, etc.
• Highly experienced working with image data and sequential data.
• Proficient in convolutional neural networks (CNN) and recurrent neural networks (RNN).
• Experienced in Large Language Models (LLM).
• Experienced in building end-to-end machine learning pipelines for production using TensorFlow extended (TFX) platform.
